# IPPAN Fees and Emission Model

This document describes the fee structure and token emission model for the IPPAN blockchain, with particular focus on AI model-related fees and economic incentives.

## Overview

The IPPAN blockchain implements a comprehensive fee and emission system designed to:
- Incentivize network participation and security
- Support AI model development and usage
- Fund governance and treasury operations
- Ensure sustainable network growth

## Token Economics

### IPPAN Token (IPN)

The IPPAN token serves multiple purposes:
- **Staking**: Securing the network through proof-of-stake consensus
- **Governance**: Voting power in governance decisions
- **Fees**: Payment for network services and AI model usage
- **Rewards**: Incentives for validators and contributors

### Token Supply

- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 IPN tokens
- **Initial Distribution**: 20% (200M tokens)
- **Staking Rewards**: 40% (400M tokens)
- **AI Model Rewards**: 20% (200M tokens)
- **Treasury**: 10% (100M tokens)
- **Development Fund**: 10% (100M tokens)

## Fee Structure

### 1. Transaction Fees

#### Base Transaction Fees

- **Simple Transfer**: 0.001 IPN
- **Smart Contract Call**: 0.01 IPN
- **Complex Operation**: 0.1 IPN
- **Governance Transaction**: 0.1 IPN

#### Dynamic Fee Adjustment

Transaction fees are adjusted based on:
- **Network Congestion**: Higher fees during high usage
- **Gas Price**: Market-driven gas pricing
- **Priority**: Users can pay higher fees for faster processing

### 2. AI Model Fees

#### Model Registration Fees

- **Base Registration Fee**: 10 IPN
- **Size-Based Fee**: 0.001 IPN per byte
- **Parameter-Based Fee**: 0.01 IPN per 1,000 parameters
- **Category Multiplier**: 
  - NLP: 1.0x
  - Computer Vision: 1.2x
  - Speech: 1.1x
  - Generative: 1.5x
  - Other: 1.0x

#### Model Execution Fees

- **Base Execution Fee**: 0.01 IPN
- **Compute-Based Fee**: 0.001 IPN per 1,000 operations
- **Memory-Based Fee**: 0.0001 IPN per MB
- **Time-Based Fee**: 0.001 IPN per second

#### Model Storage Fees

- **Daily Storage Fee**: 0.0001 IPN per MB per day
- **Minimum Storage Fee**: 0.01 IPN per day
- **Maximum Storage Fee**: 10 IPN per day

#### Model Update Fees

- **Update Fee**: 50% of registration fee
- **Size Change Fee**: 0.001 IPN per byte changed
- **Parameter Change Fee**: 0.01 IPN per 1,000 parameters changed

### 3. Governance Fees

#### Proposal Fees

- **Parameter Change**: 10 IPN
- **Treasury Spending**: 50 IPN
- **Upgrade Proposal**: 100 IPN
- **Emergency Proposal**: 500 IPN
- **AI Model Governance**: 25 IPN

#### Voting Fees

- **Vote Fee**: 0.1 IPN per vote
- **Delegation Fee**: 0.5 IPN per delegation
- **Revocation Fee**: 0.1 IPN per revocation

### 4. Network Fees

#### Validator Fees

- **Block Production**: 0.1 IPN per block
- **Attestation**: 0.01 IPN per attestation
- **Slashing**: Variable based on severity

#### Storage Fees

- **Account Storage**: 0.001 IPN per KB per month
- **Contract Storage**: 0.01 IPN per KB per month
- **AI Model Storage**: 0.0001 IPN per MB per day

## Emission Model

### 1. Staking Rewards

#### Validator Rewards

- **Base Reward**: 0.1 IPN per block
- **Stake-Based Reward**: Proportional to stake amount
- **Performance Bonus**: Additional rewards for high performance
- **Slashing Penalty**: Reduction in rewards for misbehavior

#### Delegator Rewards

- **Delegation Reward**: 80% of validator rewards
- **Validator Commission**: 20% of validator rewards
- **Compound Rewards**: Automatic reinvestment option

### 2. AI Model Rewards

#### Model Development Rewards

- **Registration Bonus**: 100 IPN for first model
- **Quality Bonus**: Up to 500 IPN for high-quality models
- **Usage Bonus**: 10% of execution fees
- **Innovation Bonus**: 1,000 IPN for breakthrough models

#### Model Usage Rewards

- **Execution Rewards**: 5% of execution fees
- **Storage Rewards**: 2% of storage fees
- **Referral Rewards**: 1% of referred user fees

### 3. Governance Rewards

#### Participation Rewards

- **Proposal Creation**: 10% of proposal fee
- **Voting Participation**: 0.1 IPN per vote
- **Delegation Management**: 0.5 IPN per delegation
- **Governance Analysis**: 1 IPN per analysis

#### Quality Rewards

- **High-Quality Proposals**: 50% bonus
- **Successful Proposals**: 100% bonus
- **Community Engagement**: Variable rewards

### 4. Treasury Allocation

#### Treasury Sources

- **Transaction Fees**: 20% of all transaction fees
- **AI Model Fees**: 30% of all AI model fees
- **Governance Fees**: 50% of all governance fees
- **Penalties**: 100% of slashing penalties

#### Treasury Usage

- **Development Grants**: 40% of treasury
- **Infrastructure**: 30% of treasury
- **Community Initiatives**: 20% of treasury
- **Emergency Reserves**: 10% of treasury
